Simple Scrambled Eggs

Ingrediants

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk or water (optional, for creaminess)
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Whisk the eggs: In a small bowl, crack the eggs and add the milk (or water) and
    salt. Vigorously whisk the mixture until the yolks and whites are uniformly combined
    with no streaks.
  2. Heat the pan:Melt the butter (or brush the pan with oil) in a nonstick skillet over
    medium heat.
  3. Cook:Pour the egg mixture into the hot skillet. Let it sit for a few seconds without
    stirring.
  4. Scramble:Use a rubber spatula to gently pull the cooked edges toward the center,
    forming large curds. Continue cooking over medium-low heat, folding and stirring
    every few seconds, allowing the uncooked egg to flow underneath.
  5. Finish:Remove the pan from the heat when the eggs are mostly set but still glisten
    with moisture. They will continue to cook slightly from the residual heat. Season with
    pepper and serve immediately.
